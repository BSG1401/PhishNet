About PhishNet

PhishNet is an advanced phishing detection platform dedicated to safeguarding users from malicious URLs. By leveraging state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms, PhishNet provides real-time analysis of URLs to determine their legitimacy, ensuring users can navigate the internet with greater confidence and security. Our mission is to create a safer digital environment by equipping users with reliable tools to identify and avoid phishing threats. We are constantly enhancing our technology to keep pace with the ever-evolving landscape of cyber threats, committed to delivering top-notch protection for our users. PhishNet utilizes a Gradient Boosting Classifier model that has been meticulously trained on a vast dataset of URLs. This model empowers the platform to accurately predict whether a URL is legitimate or a phishing attempt. Designed with a focus on user experience, PhishNet offers an intuitive interface that seamlessly integrates into everyday browsing activities.
